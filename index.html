Quicker Scan-QR reader, QR Image,QR generate

Quick Scan application is easy to scan and generate QR (Quick response) codes. Scan, save and use any information by QR Code.

Features:

*The scanned content of the code could be copied and used later.

*The scanned results are auto saved in the history along with the date.

*Any data in the history could be erased later.

*Provisions to generate QR code for plain text, url, number and contacts saved in your iPhone.

*Generated QR codes could be saved in the photo.
